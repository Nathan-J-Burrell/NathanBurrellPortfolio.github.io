about me

What began as a passionate hobby has now evolved into a dedicated career pursuit. With a profound interest in all facets of computing, I have cultivated a robust educational foundation in IT, spanning from college to university, and recently culminating in a comprehensive course with JustIT.

I am a highly motivated individual with a strong desire to continuously learn and adapt. This drive and willingness to learn permeates every aspect of my life, from gaming to forging a successful career in IT, and everything in between.

At Newcastle College, I pursued and completed an Access to HE: Computing course, where I significantly enhanced my expertise across various computing disciplines. The curriculum encompassed a diverse range of modules, including advanced communication skills, information technology systems, programming principles, organizational network security, relational database software, web design, and the creation, editing, and analysis of complex spreadsheets. This comprehensive education had equipped me with a strong foundation and versatile skill set, preparing me for where I am today.

At the University of Sunderland, I pursued a degree in Networks and Cybersecurity, where I further solidified my expertise in these critical domains. I gained hands-on experience in designing, maintaining, and securing network systems using industry-standard software such as Cisco Packet Tracer, Docker, VMware, and various other applications. Although personal circumstances prevented me from completing the course, I have the opportunity to return and finish my studies. This experience has equipped me with a basic understanding of Networks and Cybersecurity.

Recently, I completed an intensive bootcamp with JustIT, where I honed my skills in both front-end and back-end software development. Throughout the rigorous 8-week training program, I reinforced and expanded my foundational knowledge in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, and Python. Leveraging the cutting-edge techniques and best practices acquired during the bootcamp, I successfully developed this portfolio and several projects, showcasing my ability to apply theoretical knowledge to practical, real-world scenarios.
